    General questions

    What is MotorSpecs?

    MotorSpecs is a vehicle hire company with multiple car rental locations at Brisbane, Melbourne, Sydney, Adelaide, and the Gold Coast. MotorSpecs offers a wide range of cars, 4WDs and minibuses as well as Australia’s largest fleet of truck and commercial vehicles that are distributed in these locations for customers.

    How covered am I as a customer?

    Vehicles hired at any of our office locations are comprehensively insured, and our top-notch service provision aims to protect the right of our renters under the Australian Consumer Law (ACL).

    How accessible are vehicles at your store locations?

    Our nation-wide offices feature a wide range of vehicles available for immediate hire. Customers are encouraged to utilise the "view store" section of the website to confirm the availability of vehicles at a specific location.
    Vehicle Specific Questions

    How recent are the model of vehicles at your locations?

    MotorSpecs have a strong policy around the vehicles we have advertised for hire. MotorSpecs ensure that vehicles older than five years are disposed of through auctions, this is to ensure that all old vehicles are not rented out to customers as we understand our duty of care around keeping our customers safe from hazardous circumstances.

    Do I need a specific licence to hire a truck?

    Yes, since all our trucks fall under the Heavy Rigid (HR) machinery, you would be expected to have an HR manual licence to hire a truck at MotorSpecs

    How often are your rental vehicles serviced?

    All our vehicles are serviced every 5,000km to ensure the safety of our service users. Again, MotorSpecs understand its duty of care around public safety.

    Can I request for a specific make/model?

    Yes, you may reserve your preference of any of our fleet vehicles, and we’ll ensure to have that exact car waiting for you at the pick-up area. And once you're behind the wheel of your favourite rental car, if you decide to keep your vehicle for a few more days, that's no problem at all.

    Does MotorSpecs have 4WDs?

    We have an extensive fleet which includes mid-size to large SUVs, many of which offer 4WD. Kindly utilise the view cars column of our website to view the range of our vehicles including 4WDs, cars, coupe, and so on..

    Do you offer hybrid vehicles?

    MotorSpecs offer low-emmission hybrid vehicles at our various ocations. Most of our vehicles, especially cars, are eco-friendly and get great fuel mileage.
    Service Specific Questions

    What is the policy around utilising toll roads

    We encourage you to contact the toll provider after travelling the toll road - within 72 hours (Melbourne, Brisbane) or 48hours (Sydney). If you refuse to take action, a toll bill may be forwarded to the address you provided to MetroSpecs during initial registration.

    How do I modify or cancel my booking?

    You are expected to get in touch with MetroSpecs By using the contact information on the our contact page.
